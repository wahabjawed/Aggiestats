Game stats start updating approximately 15 minutes prior to game time. The previous game's data will be visible until then.

Stats are generated from the same XML feed that goes to the press box, digital scoreboards, and other destinations. Technical difficulties occasionally arise with that feed that are (unfortunately) out of our control...but if it's broken, be assured that we're doing what we can to help get it fixed!
